Float - Navigation & photography workshop

An electric skateboard with navigation system and improved safety. Which is fully functional as a moving vehicle and also a fully functional prototype. Sensors detect the inclination and turn on the indicators accordingly. With the help of detection of acceleration, we can light up the brake light to match the situation. The smaller the drop in acceleration, the slower the brake light flashes. However, if the drop is high, the brake light flashes several times quickly, warning people behind you. The following pictures were taken by us, who developed the prototype, at a photography workshop and tell a day in the life of a user of Float.
